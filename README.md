## Vision

CareerOS is an open-source AI platform that helps people discover, organize, communicate, and grow their careers through evidence-based reasoning.

Unlike traditional resume builders, CareerOS does not treat the resume as the source of truth.

Instead, CareerOS builds a structured understanding of a candidate's professional journey and generates resumes, LinkedIn profiles, interview preparation, career insights, and more from that single source of truth.

---

# Mission

Help every professional communicate their genuine value through transparent, explainable, and evidence-based AI.

# Guiding Philosophy

CareerOS is not designed to replace career professionals.

It is designed to help people better understand and communicate their own professional experiences.

Every recommendation should be grounded in evidence provided by the user.
